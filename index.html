Open Legal Issue Taxonomy (OLIT)

A SKOS controlled vocabulary of ~288,000 legal-issue concepts under 13 top-level domains. Permanent base: https://w3id.org/legal-taxonomy/

👉 Explore the taxonomy interactively — a browser-based notebook to browse domains, drill into sub-issues, and see the data.

Every concept resolves to its own page — e.g. concept/010089. Browse from the concept index (the 13 top-level domains).

Download the data

  • legal-taxonomy.ttl — full vocabulary (SKOS / Turtle)
  • legal-taxonomy.jsonld — full vocabulary (JSON-LD)
  • scheme.ttl — ConceptScheme + 13 top concepts
  • dumps/ — Turtle split by top-level domain
  • legal-taxonomy.db.gz — SQLite (all 287k concepts + source links, gzipped)
